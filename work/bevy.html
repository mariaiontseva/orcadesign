The Brief

A retreat platform that sells the experience before the booking

Bevy needed a landing page that would convert corporate decision-makers into retreat bookings. The challenge: communicate luxury, trust, and seamless logistics in a single scroll. Every section needed to reduce friction and build confidence — from the hero search bar to the experience cards grid.

Design Decisions

Strategic choices behind every pixel

Colour Strategy

Deep forest green (#1B4332) anchors trust and premium feel. Mint accent (#2ECC71) drives CTAs without competing with photography.

Typography

Clash Display for editorial hero headlines that stop the scroll. Poppins for body copy — clean, modern, high readability at all sizes.

Clash Display Poppins — The quick brown fox jumps over

Hero with Search

Embedded search bar in the hero section. Users can start browsing destinations immediately — reducing time-to-action by one full step.

Alternating Blocks

Image-text pairs zigzag down the page, maintaining visual rhythm. Each block addresses one objection: destinations, activities, logistics.
